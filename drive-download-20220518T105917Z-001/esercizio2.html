Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with caramelized onions, tart, cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday started!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500) all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onion
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoon olive oil, for brushing in top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scape rind off whit a paring knife is desider, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh of frozen cranberries

Method

Make the focaccia dough

In the medium- size mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients unitil the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need actually knead the dough, though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
